Within the time limit, Store trash falling from the sky that matches the type of trash marked in the trash bin.

| If you store the right garbage, you get a point.

| Otherwise, you lose a point if you store other garbage types.

| The type of trash can varies for every 5 points. You have to save the trash according to the changing trash bin.

| The timer is extended by 3 seconds for each 3 points.

| Donuts, a special item, have the effect of affecting the game. Each doughnut is randomly colored. Various effects can be obtained depending on the color. The effects can be beneficial or harmful.

| The effect consists of the speed and the size of the trash bin, the speed at which the trash falls, and the bonus score.

* ← ↑ → ↓(A W D S): Move trash bin
* Shift: Fall transh bin
* Spacebar: Rise transh bin
